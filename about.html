Our Story

From a small consultancy to an industry-leading forensic accounting firm — our journey has been defined by relentless pursuit of truth.

2001

Foundation

ForensicEdge was founded by Elizabeth Carter with a vision to bring unparalleled financial investigative expertise to businesses facing fraud and financial disputes.

2007

National Expansion

Opened offices in Chicago, Los Angeles, and Miami — establishing a nationwide presence and handling complex multi-state investigations for Fortune 500 companies.

2014

Digital Forensics Division

Launched our industry-leading digital forensics lab, integrating AI-driven analytics and blockchain investigation capabilities to tackle modern financial crimes.

2020

Global Reach

Expanded operations internationally with partnerships in London, Singapore, and Dubai — supporting cross-border investigations and regulatory compliance worldwide.

Present

Industry Leadership

Today, ForensicEdge stands as a trusted name with 500+ successful investigations, recognized by ACFE, AICPA, and global regulatory bodies as a leader in forensic accounting.
